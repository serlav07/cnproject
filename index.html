What we do

We showcase contemporary plays written by women. This website it's also a useful tool for networking and creating a virtual hub where young women playwrights can meet established playwrights.

Only 3 productions out 12 were written by women in the Autumn November 2018 - August 2019 season at the National Theatre.

We aim, through this website, to encourage young playwrights to write and write and write in the hope that the playwriting industry will reach gender parity by April 2025.
